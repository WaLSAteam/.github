# WaLSA Team

An international science team focused on **Waves in the Lower Solar Atmosphere** (**WaLSA**). The team aims to build a comprehensive picture of wave propagation — from generation in sub-photospheric layers, through channelling in the photosphere and chromosphere, to interaction and dissipation in the magnetised outer atmosphere — by combining **high-resolution observations** with **theoretical modelling and numerical simulations**, and by developing and sharing open tools that support robust wave and oscillation analysis.

**Website:** https://WaLSA.team

---

## What we do
- Investigate **MHD wave modes** (e.g., Alfvénic and magneto-acoustic waves) in the lower solar atmosphere  
- Bridge **observations ↔ theory/simulations** to improve interpretation and physical inference  
- Develop and share **analysis methods and tools** that enable robust wave/oscillation diagnostics  
- Support community visibility and outreach through activities such as **Image of the Month**

---

## Open-source software
We maintain and curate open tools developed within the WaLSA ecosystem:

- **WaLSAtools** — wave & oscillation analysis methods  
  https://github.com/WaLSAteam/WaLSAtools

- **WaLSAlib** — auxiliary routines that support analysis-ready products   
  https://github.com/WaLSAteam/WaLSAlib
